About Us

We Care For Your Dental Health

I'm Dr. El Ghaloussi a highly skilled dentist with years of experience providing exceptional care to patients in Tunisie. After working with several clinics in Morocco, I decided to open my own dental office in Tangier where I could offer personalized care to my patients.

With a passion for dentistry and a commitment to staying up-to-date with the latest techniques and technologies, I become a trusted name in the dental community. I take time to get to know each of my patients, listening carefully to their concerns and providing customized treatment plans that meet their unique needs.
